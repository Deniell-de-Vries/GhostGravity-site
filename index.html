About Us

We make strange games
about ordinary things.

GhostGravity is a small indie studio making cosy, illustrated adventure games that take unexpected turns. Our worlds look warm and familiar — until they aren't.

We care about characters, comedy, and the kind of story that makes you feel something you didn't see coming.
